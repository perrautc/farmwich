At Farmwich, we have a commitment to family, freshness, quality, value, and simplicity. We opened our doors in 2018 and have not looked back since.

  1. Family
    First and foremost we are a family restaurant. Our mission is to "Feed your family like we do our own."
  2. Freshness
    From the meat to the squeezed fruit juices in the bar, the experience is honest, real & fresh! Nothing is artificial. The produce is the freshest available since we purchase from local sources.
  3. Quality
    We strive for the highest quality in all things - food, beverages, service & experience. The experience for our guests must be the best it can be.
  4. Value
    We are committed to providing value to our customers. Our goal is to meet all the criteria listed here and do so within a reasonable time and moderate price structure.
  5. Simplicity
    We keep things straight-forward & simple. Customers should be comfortable & relaxed! We are not about flash & dazzle.
